Add a remote_write block to your Prometheus configuration:

  remote_write:
    - url: "http://{{ template "prometheus-appinsights-remote-write.name" . }}.default.svc.cluster.local/metrics"

View the logs from your new application to make sure it's sending telemetry:

  kubectl logs $(kubectl get pods -l app={{ template "prometheus-appinsights-remote-write.name" . }}  -o jsonpath="{.items[0].metadata.name}")
